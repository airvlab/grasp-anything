Grasp-Anything++ extend Grasp-Anything and introduces over **10 million** language-based grasping instructions. Our dataset enables **language-driven grasping** task and allows the robots to grasp specific objects based on language command.

## Method
<p align="center">
  <img src="../../assets/images/grasp-anything-pp-methodology.png" alt="method" style="width: 100%;" />
</p>
We introduce a new language-driven grasping method based on diffusion models with a new contrastive training objective.
